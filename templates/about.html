About Me

Hello, I'm NDACYAYISABA Lambert, a creative and analytical applied mathematician and software engineer with a passion for building impactful technology in autonomy, machine learning, and social innovation. Experienced in developing end-to-end tools and platforms using Python, PHP, and modern web technologies. Deeply interested in the intersection of robotics, AI, and accessibility.

I am the Co-Founder and Developer of Homelink Services, a digital platform for domestic worker services in Rwanda, aimed at digitizing the informal sector to enhance job dignity and service transparency. I am also an Innovation Grant Recipient from the HATANA Bootcamp, where I won a $5,000 grant for my pitch and prototype.

As a Youth Facilitator at NDABAGA Impact NGO, I facilitated workshops on life skills, leadership, and digital literacy. I have experience as a Web Developer for Nhonest Supermarket, building an e-commerce site with features like product catalog, user authentication, and admin dashboard.

I am deeply interested in the intersection of AI and education, and I am committed to using technology to solve real-world problems in Rwanda and beyond.
